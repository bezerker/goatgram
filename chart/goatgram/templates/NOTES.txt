To access from outside the cluster, create a Cloudflare Tunnel (cloudflared) with
an ingress rule pointing traffic to the internal service.

Add this to your cloudflared config:

---

ingress:
  - hostname: goatgram.net
    service: http://goatgram.{{ .Release.Namespace }}.svc.cluster.local:{{ .Values.service.port }}
  - hostname: www.goatgram.net
    service: http://goatgram.{{ .Release.Namespace }}.svc.cluster.local:{{ .Values.service.port }}
  - service: http_status:404

---

Then restart cloudflared and ensure DNS (CNAME) records for goatgram.net and
www.goatgram.net point to your Cloudflare Tunnel.
